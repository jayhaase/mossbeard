# Forgotten Words Script
Patron: "Wanna trade?"

Mossbeard: "Yes! I must warn you though, my trinkets are not ordinary."

_Pause for Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "My trinkets come with responsibility. Are you sure you want to continue?"

_Pause for Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "Before we start, I must ask you a question. Imagine only one person remembers a word. Everyone else has forgotten the word.

_Pause for a brief moment._

Mossbeard: "And then, that one person forgets the word. What do you think happens to the word?"

_Pause for Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "The word is forgotten. Do you know what happens to forgotten words?"

_Pause for Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "They appear in a magical box I was given a long time ago. What I can offer you today is a forgotten word. Your mission will be to bring the word back to life. Do you still want to continue?"

_Pause for Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "Before we can start, I need you to hold some of my magical items. I'm not sure why, it's just how the magic works."

_Mossbeard hands out a interesting set of magical items for the involved Patrons to hold. Mossbeard takes the magic box out of its red bag._

Mossbeard: "This is the magic box where forgotten words appear."

_Mossbeard opens the box to reveal the small scrolls of forgotten words. Each scroll contains one word._

Mossbeard: "Choose wisely, take just one each."

_Pause for Patron to take a small scroll, and then Mossbeard closes the box and puts it in his satchel._

Mossbeard: "Now that the box is put away, I can also put away the items you are holding. Thank you for taking such good care of them."

_Mossbeard received the items and puts them in his satchel._

Mossbeard: "It's time to open your words. When you reveal you word, say it loudly."

_Pause for the Patron to open their word. After a Patron reads their word Mossbeard will say, while holding his staff high:_

Mossbeard: "By moss and stone, this word is now known!"

Mossbeard: "What does your word mean?"

_Pause for the Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "You must discover its meaning. You must teach others the word."

Mossbeard: "What will happen if you forget your word?"

_Pause for the Patron to respond._

Mossbeard: "Yes, it will return back in my magic box."

## About some of the items

### Gravity Compass
- A heavy solid copper ball
- If dropped needs to be re-calibrated. 
- Can only be re-calibrated by a gnome that juggles it.
- "Do not drop it", as Mossbeard drops it into their hands
- It helps Mossbeard know up from down when doing complex magic

## Heavy Glass Bottle
- Thick glass with a cork stopper
- Contains fairy breath from Beetle the fairy
- "Do you know what is in this special bottle?"
- "Do you know what happens when you put fairy breath in a normal bottle? I don't!"

## Everwood Staff
- Has crystal in the top
- The Crystal is the heart of Fairywing Forest
- "It's heavy, don't drop it!"

## Monkey Paw
- Large copper pendent with galaxy marble inside
- "Let me know if it starts going kwee-kwee"
- "I feel happy when I hold this -- maybe it will do the same for you?"

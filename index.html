The Wanderer

✦   ✦   ✦

Long ago, the world was filled with magic — not just sparks and spells, but the magic of wonder, laughter, and words that danced on the tongue. Mossbeard the Wanderer has taken up a mission: to bring back the magic.

In the elder days, ere the world grew weary, there came forth a wanderer from the deep and shadowed woods — a wizard of no high hall nor gilded court, but of moss-covered stones and the whispering boughs of ancient trees. By many names he is known, though none hold fast; like wind they are ever changing.

He bears a staff of Everwood, crowned with crystal wrought from frost and fire, said to stir when old magic is nearby. His boots have worn the shape of many paths.

He claims no throne, yet is received with gladness where kind folk dwell. For he brings not gold nor war, but tales forgotten, laughter kindled, and a quiet wisdom born of long listening.

And so he walks still — through fields of men and merriment, where the old songs linger and wonder yet remains.

The Forgotten Words

✦   ✦   ✦

Over time, people forgot certain words — and with them, a little magic faded from the world. Those forgotten words do not simply vanish. They appear in a magical box, waiting for a new keeper.

Should you encounter Mossbeard at a faire, you may be invited to trade. But be warned — his trinkets are not ordinary, and they come with responsibility. If you are chosen to receive a Forgotten Word, your mission is simple:

Speak it. Share it. Teach it. Give it life again.

When you do, the world grows a little brighter, and the magic returns. And should you ever forget your word… it will find its way back into the box.

"By moss and stone, this word is now known!"

Relics & Curiosities

✦   ✦   ✦

Mossbeard travels with a collection of curious and powerful artifacts. Should you be trusted with one, handle it with care.

⚈

The Gravity Compass

A heavy, solid copper ball. It helps Mossbeard know up from down during complex magic — though he will drop it directly into your hands, with little warning.

❄

The Fairy Breath Bottle

A thick glass vessel with a cork stopper, containing the breath of Beetle the Fairy. What happens when you put fairy breath in a normal bottle? Even Mossbeard doesn't know.

✨

The Everwood Staff

Crowned with a crystal that is the very heart of Fairywing Forest. It stirs when old magic is near. It is heavy — do not drop it.

The Lucky Monkey Paw

A copper pendant holding a swirling galaxy marble. It tends to bring happiness to those who hold it. Let Mossbeard know if it starts going kwee-kwee.
